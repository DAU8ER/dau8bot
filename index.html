DAU8BOT
This bot is a usefull bot for custom commands/afk/timer etc. The commands that you can use and that are part of the bot are listed below. You can set your own commands in your channel, but they wont be listet on this page. If you have any questions or requests, please feel free to contact me using the options below. Discord: Dauber #0001 Twitch: Dau8er If you want to JOIN the bot in your channel, pls contact me or write in bots chat 8!join If you want to PART the bot from your channel, pls contact me or write in bots chat 8!part Default Prefix: 8!
Commands available for everyone:
Commands
Description
afk
Sets your status to afk, you'll get greeted as soon as you write in one of the bots channels.
gn
Sets your status to sleeping, response equivalent to afk command.
tuck
Tuck someone to bed.
rafk/cafk
Resume your previously set afk status.
timer <time> <message>
Sets a timer sending your message after the specified time.
deltimer <ID>
Delete on of your timer.
getemotes
Lists all enabled emotes in the channel.
getemotec <emote>
Shows up the current Emote cooldown.
ping
Info about the Bot.
kok
Exposes your (100% real) kok length.
spam <spam count> <emote/text>
Let the bot spam your stuff.
getcmds
Lists all enabled commands in the channel.
getprefix
Shows your the current channel prefix. You can use the 8! prefix to use this command.
Commands available for Moderators and the Broadcaster:
Commands
Description
addemote <emote> <cooldown>
Adds a emote to get reacted on by the Bot. (10s cooldown by default)
removeemote <emote>
Removes a emote from the Bot.
emotec/cemote <emote> <cooldown>
Sets a new Emote cooldown.
addcmd <command> <message>
There you can add your own commands to your channel. (2s cooldown by default)
editcmd <command> <message>
Update your commands.
editcmdc <command> <cooldown>
Sets a new command cooldown.
delcmd <command>
Delete one of your commands.
Commands available for the Broadcaster:
Commands
Description
setprefix <prefix >
Set your own custom channel prefix for all commands. (default Prefix always available)
delprefix
Delete your custom channel prefix.

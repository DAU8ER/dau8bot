This Bot is a useful Chat Bot for custom Commands, Emote reactions, Timers, 7tv, etc. The Commands are listed below.
You can create custom Commands in your Channel (they wont be listed on this Page).
In addition, you can now add or remove 7tv Emotes to or from a Channel and also "yoink" them from others.
If you have any questions or future Requests, feel free to Contact me: Discord: Dauber #0001 Twitch: Dau8er If you want to JOIN or PART the bot to or from your channel, contact me or use one the respective command in the bots chat Default Prefix: 8!

Commands available for Moderators and the Broadcaster:
new Commands
Description
7tvadd <emote / 7tv emote link>
Add a 7tv emote, either by Name or via a 7tv Emote Link.
7tvrm <emote>
Removes a 7tv Emote from your Channel. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvyoink <yoink channel> <emote>
Activates a 7tv Emote from the specified Channel and adds it to your Channel with the same Emote Name. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvrename <current Emote> <new Emote>
Changes a 7tv Emote Name enabled in your Channel. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvadduser <7tvusername>
Add a User who has a different Username on Twitch than on 7tv (the user's Data is then stored in a Bot-internal Database)
Commands
Description
addemote <emote> <cooldown>
Adds a Emote to get reacted on by the Bot (10s cooldown by default).
removeemote <emote>
Removes a Emote from the Bot.
emotec/cemote <emote> <cooldown>
Sets a new Emote cooldown.
addcmd <command> <message>
Adds a custom Command to your Channel (2s cooldown by default).
editcmd <command> <message>
Updates a custom Command.
editcmdc <command> <cooldown>
Sets the cooldown of a custom Command.
delcmd <command>
Deletes a custom Command.

7TV

Commands
Perms
Description
7tvaddperm <User>
Broadcaster or higher Gives a user the perms to edit 7tv emotes.
7tvdelperm <User>
Broadcaster or higher Removes a user's perms to edit your 7tv emotes.
7tvgetuser
Broadcaster or higher Shows you all your current 7tv editors.
7tvemotes <Channel>
everyone Sends a 7tv Link from the given Emote-Set, of the respective 7tv User. (leave empty, to get your Current Emote-Set)
7tvadd <emote / 7tv emote link>
Moderator or higher Add a 7tv emote, either by Name or via a 7tv Emote Link.
7tvrm <emote>
Moderator or higher Removes a 7tv Emote from your Channel. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvyoink <yoink channel> <emote>
Moderator or higher Gets a 7tv Emote from the specified Channel and adds it to your Channel with the same Emote Name. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvrename <current Emote> <new Emote>
Moderator or higher Changes a 7tv Emote Name enabled in your Channel. (7tv Links are not supported)
7tvadduser <7tvusername>
Moderator or higher Add a User who has a different Username on Twitch than on 7tv
(the user's Data is then stored in a Bot-internal Database)

Custom Commands

Commands
Perms
Description
getcmds
everyone Lists all enabled Commands in the Channel.
addcmd <command> <message>
Moderator or higher Adds a custom Command to your Channel (2s cooldown by default). $(user) and $(sender) are available now.
editcmd <command> <message>
Moderator or higher Updates a custom Command.
editcmdc <command> <cooldown>
Moderator or higher Sets the cooldown of a custom Command.
delcmd <command>
Moderator or higher Deletes a custom Command.
Keywords

Commands
Perms
Description
getkeywords
everyone Lists all enabled Keywords in the Channel.
addkeyword <keyword> ; <message>
Moderator or higher Add a Keyword. Important don't forget the Split Key ";" set after the Keyword and before the Message. (5s cooldown by default). $(user) and $(sender) are available now. Also you can use the /timeout Command in your Keywords Message.
editkeyword <keyword> ; <message>
Moderator or higher Updates a Keyword Message. Important don't forget the Split Key ";" set after the Keyword and before the Message. $(user) and $(sender) are available now. Also you can use the /timeout Command in your Keywords Message.
editkeywordc <keyword> ; <cooldown>
Moderator or higher Sets a new cooldown of a Keyword. Important don't forget the Split Key ";" set after the Keyword and before the Message.
delkeyword <keyword>
Moderator or higher Delete one of your Keywords.
